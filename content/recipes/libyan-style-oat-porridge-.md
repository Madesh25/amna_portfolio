1/2 cup ground oats
1 cup milk
A pinch of salt
1 tablespoon date syrup (date molasses)
1 teaspoon olive oil
Optional: a sprinkle of Bsisa powder (Libyan roasted grain mix)

<!-- SPLIT -->

1. In a small pot, add the ground oats, milk, and a pinch of salt.
2. Place the pot over low heat.
3. Stir continuously until the mixture thickens and becomes smooth and creamy.
4. Once it reaches a thick consistency, remove it from the heat.
5. Pour the mixture into a serving bowl.
6. Drizzle the date syrup and olive oil on top.
7. Optionally, sprinkle some Bsisa powder for extra flavor, similar to Libyan Asida.
