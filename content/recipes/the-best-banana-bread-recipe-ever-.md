ingredients: 3 ripe bananas, = 1 ½ cups mashed banana
⅓ cup brown sugar
¼ cup honey
¼ cup milk
* 1 vanilla packet
1 tsp apple cider vinegar
3 tbsp butter, melted
2 cups oat flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
½ tsp salt
⅓ cup chocolate chips, plus more for topping
¼ cup walnuts, chopped


* instructions:
Preheat the oven to 190c⁰ and line a 9×5 inch loaf pan with parchment paper.
In a large bowl, whisk together the mashed banana, brown sugar, honey, milk, melted butter, vanilla and apple cider vinegar until combined.
Mix in the oat flour, baking powder, cinnamon, salt, and chocolate chips until just combined.
Transfer the batter to the loaf pan and top with more chocolate chips and chopped walnuts (if desired). 
Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
Remove the bread from the oven and let it sit in the pan for 15 minutes. Then, transfer to a wire rack to cool completely (tempting, but this is very important!). Once cool, slice and serve!
